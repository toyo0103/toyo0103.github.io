Draft 1 My Family

Hello Sharon, I want to introduce my family to you. My family has (*1.1) 4 people, my father mother, and little brother(*1.2). Father and mother(*1.3) are a very humorous person(*1.4), they usually say a joke(*1.5) to each other and make the home(*1.6) very noisy. Every day(*1.7) I can hear laugh (*1.8) when I wake up. So I always say they are ganhwa king(*1.9). In Chinese, we call that 「幹話王」.

Let’s(*1.10) us to talk about my little brother,(*1.11) he is a nerdy boy and always stay at the home(*1.12), watch movies, Korea drama or read comic books(*1.13). He told me he has some friends, but I don’t really believe that. Because of(*1.14) I only see his friends two times, maybe they are a Extras(*1.15). He is a engineer(*1.16) too(*1.17) and like a almost engineer who doesn’t have a girlfriend(*1.18). But I think he is a good and kind person. Hope one day he can find the soulmate(*1.19).

Oh, I forgot to introduce my girlfriend Ruth. She is a cute girl with a sunny personality(*1.20). As you talk to her, you will find that(*1.21). She is a king of ganhwa(*1.22) too, but I only tell you(*1.23). And remember, this is our secret haha.

Finally, I think my English is very pool, Ruth already gives up me(*1.24), I really feel happy when I know you want to teach me English, thank you, Sharon, you are good and beautiful and kind person, in Chinese, we will say 「讚讚」 to you.(*1.25)

*1.1

句子中的主角(主詞)是「有生命」的名詞時,才可以使用 have 或 has,例如:我有汽車。湯姆有房子。Robert有兩個女兒。至於像是「一週有七天。」「這棟房子裡有五個房間。」「我的學校有三千名學生。」這類型的句子,請使用 there be的句型

My family是一個概念，那用there be( is/ are)要怎麼改？

​ Come on, Mary,let’s dace! （瑪莉，我們來跳舞吧）

​ Let us : 不包含說話對象

​ Father, let us marry ! (父親，讓我們結婚吧！)

怎樣是完整的句子？你如果不會判斷的話，你可以用檢查的方式

先看一下句型介紹

基本上一個完整的句子只會有一個主詞跟一個動詞。

所以說如果你發現你一個很長的句子不確定文法是不是正確，你就檢查一下整句有沒有多出主詞，或出現超過一個動詞？有的話代表文法可能有誤，或者是需要用別種連接詞才能讓他變成正確的句子…因為現在程度還不夠，所以最好就是把每個完整句子都斷句(用句號)，或者最多只用各種連接詞(比如and/ so/ because這種基本的連接詞)來組合兩個完整句子。

*2.1

用there is/ there are 句型寫。通常要表示家族有幾個人都是用這種句型，不會拿家族來當主詞。通常說明一個東西裡面有幾個的時候都是用這種句型，比如「家裡有兩台筆電」、「鉛筆盒裡有三隻筆」之類的。

*2.2

去查一下include/ including/ included的用法，然後把這句寫正確。

1. **include** : 就是一般動詞「**包括、包含**」的意思，需要搭配主詞、受詞。    
2. **including** : 是用來帶出包含的事物，在主要句子後面先加上逗號之後，由 including 引導含括的事物。
3. **included** : 也是在主要句子後加上逗號，再作補充說明，不過它放置的位子跟 including 相反，會先講出所包含的事物，再加上 included 於後方位置。

下面是練習，請造句：

日文考試有三部分，包括閱讀、聽力跟寫作。 (including)

​ There are three parts of Japanese exam, including reading, listening, and writing.

這金額已經包含稅金了。(included)

​ This price is tax included.

這套餐包含前菜、煮餐，還有甜點。 (includes)

​ This meal includes appetizer, main course, and dessert.

​

Draft 3 My Family

Hello Sharon, I want to introduce my family to you. There are 4 people, including my father, mother, little brother, and me. My Father and mother are very humorous. They usually joke with each other and make our home very noisy. Every day I can hear their laughs when I wake up. So I always say they are king of ganhwa to them. In Chinese, the king of ganhwa means someone always talks trash.

Let’s talk about my little brother. He is a nerdy boy and he always stays at home. He often watches movies and Korean dramas. Sometimes he reads comic books. He told me he has some friends, but I don’t really believe that because I only saw his friends two times. Maybe those people whom I only saw twice are actors or actresses. Like me, he is an engineer. Like most engineers, he doesn’t have a girlfriend. But I think he is a good and kind person. I hope one day he will find his soulmate.

Oh, I forgot to introduce my girlfriend Ruth. If you talk to her, you will know that she is a cute and sunny girl. Let me tell you one secret. She is also a king of ganhwa. But please don’t tell anyone or she will be very angry. This is our secret haha.

Finally, I think my English is very poor. Ruth already gave me up. I feel really happy when I know you are willing to teach me English. Thank you, Sharon. You are a good, beautiful, and kind person. In Chinese, we will say “讚讚” to you.
